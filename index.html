Our History

Since 1990

Our bakeries have a long history and are loved by many consumers. Our bakers are experienced and highly skilled professionals who take care to make every type of bread and bring you a healthy and delicious enjoyment. Our bakery started in the 1990s, when we had a small bakery to supply the daily needs of local residents. After years of development and growth, our bakery has become one of the local well-known brands, deeply loved and trusted by the majority of consumers.

We have been adhering to the "quality first, service first" business philosophy, continuous innovation and progress, in order to meet consumer needs and expectations. We offer a wide range of breads and pastries in different flavours for a different culinary experience. We also have a healthy line of bread such as gluten-free and organic.
